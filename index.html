About Me

Hi, I'm Juan, originally from Madrid, Spain. My curiosity and love for exploring new places brought me to London in 2012. After living in various neighborhoods across this vibrant city, I eventually settled in South London, where I found my home.

My Education

Fueled by my passion for music and sound, I pursued a degree in Telecommunications Engineering with a specialization in Image and Sound. This foundation allowed me to explore the intersection of technology and creativity. I went on to complete a Master's degree in Telecommunications, where I took my creative skills further by developing a video game for my final project using Pure Data (PD).

My Culinary Journey

When I moved to London, my love for cooking led me to start working as a chef. I developed my skills in various kitchens, including Jamie's Italian and Evolve Hospitality, learning from different teams and growing through hands-on experience in the fast-paced world of hospitality.

My Transition to Development

I wanted to do something more creative that also connected with my background in technology. I began learning web development, combining my technical knowledge with a passion for building projects. Through focused learning and hands-on experience, I transitioned into working as a developer, where I found a fulfilling way to blend problem-solving with creativity.
